Our Process

1. Research

We explored feminist studies, labour reports, and workplace inequality research surrounding precarious employment and gendered emotional labour.

2. Analysis

Through thematic analysis, we identified patterns of invisible labour, unstable income, emotional burden, and unequal workplace expectations.

3. Intervention

Our intervention uses digital storytelling and visual communication to raise awareness about gender inequality in precarious labour systems.
